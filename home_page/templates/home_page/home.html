Our Venue

The Cocktail Bar

The Stunning Cocktail Bar is the jewel in MegaRestaurant. It is a breathtaking design that would sit in any list of the top bars of the world. It can be booked out for all types of events. Late nights start here… Boasting a full bar, private restrooms, grand piano and cloakroom in elegantly decorated surroundings.

Restaurant

The main restaurant offers a warm welcome to anyone who enters the building. Its bartenders will always greet you with a smile and be on hand to serve your drink of choice from its vast selection. It has an appeal of a chic French or New York bar set in Dublins city centre that is mesmerising on arrival.

The Terrace Bar

The Terrace Bar is the party bar complete with its own Dj set up and sound system. It overlooks the outdoor terrace that hosts all our outdoor events. It can be hired out exclusively for up to 250 guests or as part of The Library and Cocktail Bar to add extra capacity. The bookings team can put together your perfect combination event utilising all of the spaces.
